Comprehensive support services

We connect individuals with disabilities to essential services, technologies, and resources that enhance independence and participation.

Assistive Technology

Tools and devices that help individuals perform tasks they might otherwise be unable to do

  • Screen readers & magnifiers
  • Speech recognition software
  • Alternative input devices
  • Environmental controls

Personal Support

Services that provide direct assistance and support for daily living activities

  • Personal care attendants
  • Sign language interpreters
  • Job coaches
  • Transportation assistance

Educational Support

Resources and accommodations for academic success at all educational levels

  • Academic accommodations
  • Tutoring services
  • Assistive learning technology
  • Transition planning

Employment Services

Support for finding, obtaining, and maintaining meaningful employment

  • Vocational rehabilitation
  • Job placement assistance
  • Workplace accommodations
  • Career counseling

Housing Support

Resources for accessible and independent living arrangements

  • Accessible housing resources
  • Home modification grants
  • Independent living centers
  • Supported living services

Health Services

Healthcare and wellness services tailored to disability needs

  • Specialized healthcare
  • Therapy services
  • Mental health support
  • Nutrition counseling
